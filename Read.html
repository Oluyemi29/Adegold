HOW TO CANCEL YOUR ORDER

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Can I cancel my order?

Yes, you can cancel your order when it is in Order In Progress. Please note that orders in shipped status cannot be canceled.

My order was prepaid, how will I get a refund?

If your order is prepaid, Your refund will be process into your payment bank card/ bank account. It may take up to 3 - 7 business days for your bank to credit the refund amount to your account
If you have used a Jumia/SureGift voucher, the value will be added back to your voucher code within 24hours. The voucher code can be reused to purchase.

My order has been shipped but I still want to cancel it.

If your order has been shipped, not to worry, once you are contacted by our delivery agent, kindly reject the delivery of the shipped order.
